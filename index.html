Introduction

Hello,

My name is Graham Fox. I am 24 years old from Bray, Co.Wicklow. I'm fluent in both English and Irish. I am currently studying to become a Full Stack Developer in the Code Institute, by the end of which I will be rewarded with a Level 5 Diploma in Software Development. Earlier in the year I also received a level 6 Webmaster Diploma at IACT. I have spent the past few years working in a restaurants and studying Applied Psychology in IADT. I have chosen to pursue a career in Web Development as it is my passion to build Websites and Apps.

I have a natural eye for attention to detail, I am adaptable in dynamic environments but I feel my key advantage over my counterparts is my ability to problem solve. I am currently looking for a new and challenging career, one which will make best use of my existing skills and experience and develop them further.
